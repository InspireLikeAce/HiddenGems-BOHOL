Leave No Trace

  • • Pack out all trash you bring in
  • • Stay on designated trails and paths
  • • Do not pick plants or disturb wildlife
  • • Use biodegradable products when possible
❤️

Respect Local Culture

  • • Ask permission before taking photos of people
  • • Dress modestly when visiting churches and sacred sites
  • • Learn basic Filipino or Bisaya greetings
  • • Follow local customs and traditions
🛍️

Support Small Vendors

  • • Buy from local markets and sari-sari stores
  • • Hire local guides instead of external tour operators
  • • Stay in locally-owned accommodations
  • • Purchase handmade souvenirs directly from artisans
🗑️

Avoid Littering

  • • Bring reusable water bottles and bags
  • • Properly dispose of waste in designated areas
  • • Participate in beach or trail clean-ups if available
  • • Report illegal dumping to local authorities

SDG Alignment

This project aligns with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals — particularly SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities), SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption), SDG 14 (Life Below Water), and SDG 15 (Life on Land).
