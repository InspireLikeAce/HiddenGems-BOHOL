Project Purpose

HiddenGems Bohol is a digital showcase dedicated to promoting lesser-known natural, cultural, and community-based destinations in the province of Bohol, Philippines. Our goal is to encourage sustainable tourism by shifting attention from overcrowded tourist spots to undiscovered gems that deserve recognition and responsible visitation.

Vision & Mission

Vision

A Bohol where every community benefits from tourism, and every destination is treated with respect and care.

Mission

To digitally document and promote Bohol's hidden gems while educating visitors about responsible and sustainable tourism practices.
